## Overview

The Blackbox Web App is the fastest way to access our powerful suite of AI coding tools. It provides a clean, intuitive interface for AI chat, code generation, and project management, all from the comfort of your favorite web browser. Whether you're on a shared computer or just need a quick coding assistant, the Web App is always just a click away.

## Key Features

- **Instant Access:** Simply log in to your account and start coding.
- **Full-Fledged AI Chat:** Engage in detailed conversations, ask complex coding questions, and get instant explanations.
- **Code Generation:** Generate everything from simple functions to complex algorithms in over 20 programming languages.
- **Project Context:** Upload files or entire folders to give the AI context about your project for more accurate and relevant responses.
- **Cross-Platform:** Works seamlessly on any operating system with a modern web browser, including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

## Getting Started

1. **Navigate to the Web App:** Open your browser and go to [blackbox.ai](https://www.blackbox.ai/).
2. **Sign In:** Log in with your Blackbox account credentials.
3. **Start the Conversation:** Type your coding question or prompt into the chatbox and press Enter.
4. **Upload Files (Optional):** Click the attachment icon to upload files and provide more context for your task.

The Web App is the perfect starting point for harnessing the power of Blackbox AI.

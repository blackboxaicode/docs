BlackboxAI Encrypted is designed to meet stringent regulatory requirements across industries. This document outlines how our technical controls support compliance with major regulations and standards.

## Overview

Unlike traditional cloud AI services that rely on policies and promises, BlackboxAI Encrypted provides **technical controls** that enable compliance through:

- **Cryptographic Guarantees**: Mathematical proof of data protection
- **Hardware Attestation**: Verifiable security state
- **Zero Data Retention**: No persistent storage of sensitive data
- **Audit Trail**: Cryptographic evidence for compliance verification

## HIPAA (Healthcare)

### Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act

**Applicability**: Healthcare providers, health plans, healthcare clearinghouses, and their business associates.

### Technical Safeguards

| HIPAA Requirement | BlackboxAI Encrypted Implementation | Status |
|-------------------|-------------------------------------|--------|
| **Access Controls** | Cryptographic key-based access, only authorized clients can decrypt | ✅ Compliant |
| **Audit Controls** | Hardware attestation provides cryptographic audit trail | ✅ Compliant |
| **Integrity Controls** | ECDSA signatures ensure data integrity | ✅ Compliant |
| **Transmission Security** | TLS 1.2+ with end-to-end encryption | ✅ Compliant |
| **Encryption at Rest** | No data at rest (memory-only processing) | ✅ Compliant |
| **Encryption in Transit** | AES-256-GCM + TLS encryption | ✅ Compliant |

**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):**
- ✅ No data retained to erase

- ✅ No backups or copies

**Right to Data Portability:**
- ✅ Users control their data
- ✅ Data never leaves user's control
- ✅ Easy export from client

**Right to Access:**
- ✅ Users have complete access to their data
- ✅ No hidden processing or storage

**Right to Rectification:**
- ✅ Users control data accuracy

BlackboxAI Encrypted implements privacy by design:

- **Encryption by Default**: All data encrypted automatically
